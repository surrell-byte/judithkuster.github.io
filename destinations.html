Travel Tips & Best Times to Visit

Best Time for Safari

June to October is ideal for wildlife viewing as animals gather around water sources. The Great Migration peaks in July-August.

Beach Destinations

December to March offers perfect beach weather in Zanzibar and Mauritius. Avoid April-May rainy season.

Adventure Activities

Victoria Falls is best visited from February to July when water flow is spectacular. Gorilla trekking is year-round.
